About the Pop Up Learning Lab






The Pop Up Learning Lab is an eLearning platform by the City of Gainesville. We create engaging learning experiences about city services. Have fun while you learn about riding a bus, zoning laws, requesting services, and more.




Why did we do this? We noticed that the city got the same questions over and over about some of our services. We picked 6 topics and then partnered with student design teams to help us explain how these services work. The result? Quick fun games where you Learn, Play, Do!




Where do we get ideas for our games? From you! Have an idea for a topic? Want to design a game? Email us at DesignGNV@CityOfGainesville.org
